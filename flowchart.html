Thesis Intelligence System

Layer 1 — Broad Conviction

Warfare and security are becoming autonomous, distributed and software-defined (sensors)

Layer 2 — Impact Areas
1.a — Unmanned Systems

Attritable unmanned systems across air, land, sea & subsea

Will structurally complement and partially displace legacy platforms

1.b — Energy & Logistics

Energy density and field logistics are performance bottlenecks

Critical constraints limiting range, endurance and operational tempo of unmanned fleets

1.c — Counter-Unmanned

Counter-unmanned detection & neutralization as a permanent defensive layer

Both kinetic and non-kinetic defence capabilities will be essential

1.d — HMI & Autonomy

Low-latency, high-trust HMI and supervised autonomy frameworks

Currently underserved — decreasing operator load as fast as possible with technology
